A multinational investment bank conducts a comprehensive AI governance assessment before deploying credit scoring models and algorithmic trading systems, ensuring regulatory compliance across multiple jurisdictions.

1. Regulatory Framework Analysis

Framework Management Tools help compliance teams identify and organize applicable regulatory requirements across GDPR, SOC2, MiFID II, and Basel III, providing structured guidance for comprehensive coverage assessment.

2. Risk Assessment & Gap Analysis

Risk Management & Cross-Framework Analysis Tools assist teams in evaluating AI model risks, identifying compliance gaps, and mapping control requirements across different regulatory frameworks.

3. Control Implementation Planning

Mitigation Control Tools support the development of comprehensive mitigation strategies and help teams search for relevant controls, enabling informed decision-making for compliance implementation.

4. Documentation & Reporting

Compliance Analysis & Data Export Tools enable teams to generate structured compliance documentation and assessment reports for regulatory submissions and internal governance reviews.
